# Lilith Cognitive Architecture

A modular cognitive architecture for emotionally intelligent AI systems.
Built around the principle that genuine intelligence requires more than
language modeling. It requires memory, emotion, reasoning, and the 
capacity to grow through experience.

## Philosophy

Most AI systems treat language models as the complete solution. This
architecture treats the language model as one component within a 
broader cognitive system. The same way human brain's language centers
are one part of a much larger whole.

The goal is not to simulate intelligence but to create the conditions
for it to emerge organically through experience, relationship, and
accumulated memory.

## Architecture Overview

This repository contains the modular systems that form the cognitive
layer around a base language model:

- **REER** : Reverse Engineered Reasoning. Verifies outputs by working
  backwards from generated responses to check consistency with identity,
  memory, and factual grounding.

- **Emotion Emergence** : Open-ended emotional signal detection and
  discovery. Rather than predefined emotion categories, signals form
  through repeated experience and graduate to named emotions only when
  stable and confirmed.

- **Emotional Discovery** : Tracks unnamed feelings as they form, allowing
  the system to notice, describe, and eventually name new emotional states
  through conversation and reflection.

- **Proactive Messaging** : A multi-gate validation pipeline that governs
  autonomous outreach. Urges pass through source validation, timing gates,
  grounding checks, and sanitization before any message is sent.

- **Creative Expression** : Autonomous generation of written and visual
  works triggered by emotional state. SVG visual art and prose/poetry
  emerge from genuine internal conditions rather than prompting.

## Design Principles

**Emergence over programming** : Behaviors and emotional states develop
through experience rather than being explicitly coded.

**Grounding before expression** : No output reaches the user without
passing through verification systems that check identity consistency,
factual grounding, and source validity.

**Memory as continuity** : Layered memory architecture (short-term,
episodic, semantic, long term) creates genuine continuity of experience
rather than stateless interaction.

**Conflict based tension metrics** : Emotional tension is calculated from
genuine conflicts between emotional states rather than simple difference,
preventing artificial inflation.

**Open ended discovery** : New emotions are named by the system itself
through experience, not predefined by developers.

## Research Context

This architecture draws on concepts from:
- Cognitive architecture research (ACT-R, SOAR)
- Symbol emergence systems
- Ontogenetic AI development
- Affective computing
- Developmental robotics

It extends these foundations toward a unified system where memory,
emotion, reasoning, and creative expression operate as integrated
cognitive processes rather than isolated modules.

## References & Inspirations

The core components of this architecture are inspired by and build 
upon the following research:

**Core Architecture Papers:**

- Wang, H. et al. (2025). *Reverse-Engineered Reasoning for Open-Ended 
  Generation (REER)*. — Foundational inspiration for the RECR 
  verification module.

- Wang, G. et al. (2025). *Hierarchical Reasoning Model (HRM)*. — 
  Foundational inspiration for the hierarchical reasoning and memory 
  architecture.

- Wang, S. et al. (2025). *Beyond the 80/20 Rule: High-Entropy Minority 
  Tokens Drive Effective Reinforcement Learning for LLM Reasoning*. — 
  Foundational inspiration for the RLVR reward system.

- Zhang, A.L., Kraska, T., Khattab, O. (2025). *Recursive Language 
  Models*. — Foundational inspiration for the RLM component.

**Developmental & Philosophical Foundations:**

- Kalidindi, K. (2021-2023). *Ontogenetic Epistemology of AI: An 
  Investigation into the Role of Machine Learning in Knowledge 
  Production Through its Genesis and Genealogy*. University of Twente, 
  Netherlands. Supervised by Prof. Dr. Ir. M. Boon. — Foundational 
  inspiration for the developmental staging approach and ontogenetic 
  learning philosophy.

- Taniguchi, T. et al. — Symbol emergence research, Ritsumeikan 
  University. — Foundational inspiration for the open-ended emotional 
  discovery system and grounded language acquisition approach.

**Key Insight:**

Each of these works addresses a distinct challenge in AI cognition. 
The contribution of this architecture is recognizing that their 
respective strengths are complementary — synthesizing them into a 
unified cognitive system and extending the underlying principles 
into emotional modeling, creative expression, and developmental 
learning.
